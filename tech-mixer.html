About the Fall Tech Mixer

The Annual Tech Mixer is an event organized by Cal Poly Pomona’s Management Information Systems Student Association (MISSA) that aims to connect professionals in the tech industry with enthusiastic students interested in exploring potential career paths in Computer Science and Computer Information Systems. We hope to bridge the gap between academia and industry, helping students gain real-world perspectives and guidance as they prepare to enter the workforce–and subsequently, for professionals to engage with the next generation of talent.

Network with participating companies
Learn about possible fields and career paths
