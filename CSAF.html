What is the Cybersecurity and Awareness Fair?

The Cal Poly Pomona Cybersecurity and Awareness Fair (CSAF) is a premier event that promotes cybersecurity education, innovation, and career development. It brings together students, industry professionals, and community leaders through vendor exhibits, interactive demonstrations, student contests, and expert presentations. CSAF fosters collaboration, raises cybersecurity awareness, and connects emerging talent with leading organizations in the field.

    MISSA Express (2025)

    A ransomware crisis rocks the global Chinese-American chain “MISSA Express.” Five suspects—an Intern, Security Analyst, Contractor, Ex-Employee, and an Outsider with ties to the CEO—each have motive and opportunity. Participants sifted through real-style log files, traced digital breadcrumbs, and identified the insider who planted the ransomware before the timer ran out.
