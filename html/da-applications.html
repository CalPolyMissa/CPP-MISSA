MISSA's Data Analytics Team

The main goal of the MISSA Data Analytics team is to provide an environment for individuals interested in data analytics to collaborate, share resources, and work together. We plan to provide the opportunity for team members to work together and participate in competitions, hackathons, and research projects, as well as the opportunity to host workshops. Time commitment is up to you: will vary depending on available events, projects, & level of commitment.
