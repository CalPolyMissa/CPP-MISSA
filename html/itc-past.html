Information Technology Competition - 2023

The Information Technology Competition (ITC) is a student-led competition where teams are given case studies to research and find solutions to. Teams are given the chance to present their works to industry professionals and claim victory!

Cal Poly MISSA recently hosted the 27th annual IT Competition in 2023, and will host the 28th annual ITC in Spring 2024. Congratulations to the winning teams!
