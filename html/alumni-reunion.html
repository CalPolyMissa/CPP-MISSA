Calling all MISSA Alumni!

Join us for MISSA's 54th Annual Alumni Reunion!

6 pm - 10 pm on April 26th, 2025

Kellogg West Conference Center and Hotel 3801 West Temple Avenue Bldg. 76 Pomona, CA 91768
Sign up here!

Reconnect & Relive:
  • Join us for a special evening of nostalgia, networking, and new connections as we bring together alumni and professionals from Cal Poly Pomona's MISSA for our 54th Alumni Reunion! Reconnect with old friends, reminisce about your college days, and expand your professional network in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
Event Highlights:
  • Keynote Address: Hear from an esteemed MISSA alumnus about the future of technology and its impact on the industry.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow alumni and current members to foster professional relationships and mentorship opportunities.
  • Club Updates: Learn about MISSA's latest achievements, initiatives, and plans for the future.
  • Dinner and Entertainment: Enjoy a catered dinner and live entertainment to celebrate our incredible community.

Agenda

6:00 PM - 7:00 PM - Mocktail Mingling

  • Non-alcoholic beverages served

7:00 PM - 9:00 PM - Interactive Dinner

  • Keynote Address: MISSA President Ally Nguyen, Emeritus Professor Dr. Dan Manson, ITC Sponsor & Case Writer Jim Savitz, Executive in Residence for Cybersecurity Diane Miller
  • Table Games: Test your knowledge on MISSA Facts and History
  • Raffle: Win a CPP merchandise bundle, lunch with the current MISSA president, and more

9:00 PM - 9:45 PM - Past & Present Networking

  • Supervised networking session with all generations of MISSA

9:45 PM - 10:00 PM - Closing

  • Closing remarks; contact information and links for guests provided
