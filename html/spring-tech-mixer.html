Spring Tech Mixer 2021

With the success of our Fall 2020 Professional Tech Mixer and all the amazing feedback we received. MISSA has decided to host another tech mixer and would like for you to participate! Regardless of where you stand professionally or academically, our mixer has an opportunity for you!

The Spring Tech Mixer will be on April 23, 2021, 6 - 7:30 PM, an event where students can build their network and find opportunities to explore careers within their field. Join us on Zoom to network with industry professionals!


Companies attending

Benefits

  • Gain exposure and build connections
  • Network with the many companies participating
  • An opportunity for students to learn about the companies
  • Possibility of being offered internships and job opportunities

For More Information

If you have questions or if your company would like to participate, please contact our Director of Events.
