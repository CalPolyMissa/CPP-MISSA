Cyber Security and Awareness Fair

The Cyber Security and Awareness Fair(CSAF) on October 29, 2020 from 10:00 a.m. - 2 p.m., is an annual event where different CPP clubs and students from other schools raise awareness about cybersecurity related topics and how to protect against cyber attacks

For this year's CSAF event, our webmaster, Christian Falucho, will be presenting his project, Under the Hood. This project will be talking about phishing attacks during the COVID-19 and how to better protect yourself against them. If you are interesting in attending this event, please register through the Eventbrite below. Everyone is welcome, we hope to see you at the event.
